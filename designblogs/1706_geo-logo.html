THE GEOMETRIC LOGO

This design represents both my design principles and aesthetic. For the base shape I chose an icosahedron as I've always liked that the complexity of geomtric figures are created using simple shapes. I use this design principle as a guide for all my work.


The icosahedron has been stylised as sketch drawing. I decided to draw in this style for two reasons. Firstly, because it shows off the simple construction of this shape. And secondly, I thought that it draws parallels to the wireframes and mockups created in the discovery and development stages of web and app design.


The sketch style continues on in the text portion of the logo, and actually shaped the design of this website.
